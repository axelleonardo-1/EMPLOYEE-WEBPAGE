How to Create an Effective Resume

A well-prepared Curriculum Vitae (CV) is crucial for making an excellent first impression on employers. Here we show you how to do it correctly:


Personal and Contact Information

Include your name, email address, and phone number.

Professional Profile

A brief introduction about your objectives and qualifications.

Work Experience

List your previous jobs, responsibilities, and achievements.

Education

Detail your studies and any other relevant training.

Skills

List the skills that make you ideal for the position.

Interests

(Optional) Share your hobbies if they are relevant to the job.

References

Available upon request.
